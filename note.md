## A Brief owerview about Dataset

The dataset consists of 2,823 rows and 25 columns.
The ORDERDATE column was originally in object format and was converted to datetime format.
Missing values: ADDRESSLINE2 (71%), STATE (29%), POSTALCODE (15%) – were filled with "Unknown".
SALES column statistics: min = 482, max = 14,082, mean = 3,553, median = 3,184.
92.6% of orders have the status "Shipped".
No duplicate rows were found.
The highest number of orders was placed in 2004 (1,351 orders).
